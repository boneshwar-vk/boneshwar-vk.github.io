  1. Boneshwar, V. K., Deepesh Agarwal, Bala Natarajan, and Babji Srinivasan. "Inductive graph neural network framework for imputation of single-cell RNA sequencing data." Computers & Chemical Engineering 195 (2025): 109031 (PDF)
    Show abstract

    Abstract

    Single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) enables fine-grained analysis of cellular processes but suffers from sparsity and noise. We present an inductive graph learning framework that constructs node embeddings from subgraphs, improving scalability while achieving strong imputation and clustering performance across multiple scRNA-seq datasets. The method demonstrates gains in Silhouette and ARI scores with reduced runtime and error, supporting downstream applications in trajectory inference and pathway analysis.

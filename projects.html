Language‑Agnostic Speaker Identification & Diarisation; Transcription & Translation

Jan 2024 – May 2024

Multilingual speaker ID + diarisation with downstream transcription/translation using fine‑tuned models.

MaskedAlign: Multiple Sequence Alignment using Transformer

Jan 2024 – May 2024

Masked transformer for MSA that improves alignment quality while reducing runtime overhead.

Developed a seq‑to‑seq masked transformer (inspired by Masked Trajectory Transformer and BetaAlign) to enhance MSA generalization. PyTorch implementation beat strong baselines and cut runtime by up to 13% via decoding refinements and efficient attention. Ablations on masking schedules and positional schemes.

Synthetic Medical and IR Image Generation

Nov 2023 – Mar 2024

Diffusion + edge‑guided GAN pipelines for realistic medical/thermal IR imagery and better separability.

Industrial project (CVRDE, DRDO). Enhanced diffusion models with MixNMatch and latent controls for improved class separability/quality. Built RGB→Thermal IR translation using edge‑guided GANs to preserve boundaries and highlights. Improved downstream detection stability and reduced data collection needs.

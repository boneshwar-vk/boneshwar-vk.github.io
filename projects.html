MaskedAlign: Multiple Sequence Alignment using Transformer

Jan 2024 – May 2024

Masked transformer for MSA that improves alignment quality while reducing runtime overhead.

Proposed a seq-to-seq masked transformer (inspired by Masked Trajectory Transformer/BetaAlign) that generalizes to unseen gene families. Achieved higher alignment accuracy than classic tools (ClustalW/MAFFT) with up to 13% runtime reduction.

Latent-Code-Guided GANs for Image Generation

Nov 2023 – Mar 2024

Diffusion & edge-guided GANs for better foreground and background separability.

Industrial project (CVRDE, DRDO). Fine-tuned GANs to disentangle latent factors (shape/size/texture/color) for precise control and clean foreground–background separation. Added RGB→Thermal-IR translation via edge-guided GANs, improving foreground fidelity and yielding a 6% accuracy lift on a custom set.

Traffic Bot: Real-time Violation Detection

Jun 2022 – Feb 2023

Built an end-to-end YOLOv5 pipeline for helmet, triple-riding, and overspeed detection; achieved F1 = 0.79 and 89% deployment accuracy on Jetson Nano. Incubated at Nirmaan and integrated with existing CCTV infrastructure for real-time tracking.

✦ About

Hi, I'm Boneshwar. I build interpretable, robust models that generalize across biological modalities, from graph neural networks for single-cell genomics to generative and knowledge-grounded systems for clinical reasoning. My goal is to make AI a dependable instrument for personalized medicine.

Research interests: graph neural networks, single-cell genomics, generative modeling, knowledge graphs, topological data analysis, and LLMs for healthcare.

✦ Research

What I work on

My research sits at the intersection of representation learning and biology: designing models that are accurate, efficient, and interpretable enough to trust in a clinical setting.

i.

Single-cell genomics

Inductive graph neural networks for imputation, clustering, and trajectory inference on sparse, noisy scRNA-seq atlases.

ii.

Generative & structured models

Graph transformers, VAEs, and masked transformers that capture position and structure relationships in biological sequences and networks.

iii.

Knowledge-grounded AI

Ontology and knowledge-graph grounded LLMs for interpretable, traceable clinical decision support and differential diagnosis.
